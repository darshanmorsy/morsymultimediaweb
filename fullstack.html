Full-Stack Development

Full stack development refers to the practice of developing both the front-end and back-end of a web application. This includes working with various technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, database management, server-side scripting, and more. A full stack developer is skilled in both front-end and back-end technologies and is responsible for developing, deploying, and maintaining a complete web application. Full stack development has become increasingly popular due to its efficiency and versatility, allowing developers to work on all aspects of a project and create seamless, integrated web applications.
