Front-End Designing

UI (user interface) and UX (user experience) design are two closely related disciplines that focus on creating enjoyable and effective experiences for users of digital products. UI design involves the visual and interactive elements of a product, such as its layout, colors, typography, and icons. UX design, on the other hand, focuses on the overall usability and experience of the product, including user research, prototyping, testing, and feedback. The ultimate goal of UI and UX design is to create products that are intuitive, easy to use, and visually appealing, resulting in a positive experience for the end-user.
